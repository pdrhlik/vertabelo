# What is tf-idf

term freqeuncy
$tf = number\ of\ terms\ in\ a\ document$

inverse document frequency
$idf = ln(\frac{n_{documents}}{n_{documents\ containing\ term}})$

tf-idf
$tf\_idf = tf * idf$
